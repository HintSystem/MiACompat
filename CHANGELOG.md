## 📖 Relic compendium
Press `C` in-game to open the Relic Compendium screen

- Displays every relic available on the server
  - Discovered relics are highlighted with a colored border
  - Crafted and event relics are always visible, as they don't drop from Praying Skeletons
- Shows the drop chance for each layer, even for undiscovered relics
- Relics are categorized by grade.
- Relics are sorted by layer, then by name.

### Changes

- Items are now identified by their prefab ID instead of their item model
  - Fixes cases where different items sharing the same model could be identified incorrectly.
